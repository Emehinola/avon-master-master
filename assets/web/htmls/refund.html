In non-emergency situations, kindly contact Avon HMO should you require assistance at any time. Please, always contact the HMO to notify us of your intent to make payment out of pocket for any reasons. Do not make any payment without pre-notification to the HMO.

To enable us process your refund, we would need:

  • Comprehensive medical report(s) showing all drugs given; tests / procedures carried out; admission dates and diagnosis.

  • Detailed receipt(s) of payment or itemized hospital invoice clearly showing the cost and breakdown of the services rendered.

  • Refund code (which will be provided by our call centre once the HMO is pre-notified of your intent to make payment out of pocket. Always ensure you apply for a refund with the refund code given).

The required documents must be sent to Avon HMO no later than 30 days from encounter date. Kindly note that refund would be denied if all the above requirement(s) are not met.

Disclaimer: Please note that all refunds are subject to investigation and review. In all instances, the
refund being claimed must be in respect of medical services that are covered by the health plan. Any refund requests for services accessed outside the HMO network providers, will be processed based on the amount we would have paid your primary provider or any provider within your plan access.
